Q&A

  • 1. Is Orbiter's Visa / Master a credit card or a debit card?

    Orbiter's Visa / Master is a prepaid card. Prepaid cards are the same as debit cards. The difference is that debit cards are linked to your bank account, but prepaid cards need to be topped up. In our product, you can recharge with cryptocurrency.

  • 2. How to apply for a Orbiter's Visa / Master card?

    First of all, you can register Orbiter with your mobile phone number or email address, download the APP and complete KYC according to the prompted steps. Generally, the system will take 2 minutes to check. After passing the application, you can click the application on the home page of the APP, and you will immediately receive a Orbiter's Visa / Master card.

  • 3. How do I recharge my Orbiter's Visa / Master card?

    You can use cryptocurrencies in the Orbiter app to convert to USD, such as USDT, and then recharge the converted USD into a Orbiter's Visa / Master card to make global payment.
